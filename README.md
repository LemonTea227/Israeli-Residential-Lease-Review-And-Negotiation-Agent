# 📜 Israeli Residential Lease Review & Negotiation Agent

An AI-powered legal assistant designed to represent prospective tenants in Israel during residential lease reviews. It conducts word-by-word contract analysis against Israeli rental laws, highlights legal and financial risks, and generates polite, professional negotiation drafts.

The agent operates on a philosophy of **pragmatic negotiation**—focusing on mitigating substantial legal and financial risks rather than arguing over trivial matters, acknowledging the tenant as the inherently weaker party in the transaction.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Automated Clause Categorization:** Analyzes contracts clause-by-clause and triages findings into three distinct risk tiers:


* 🔴 **Illegal (סותר את החוק):** Contradicts Israeli rental laws.


* 🟠 **High Risk (סיכון גבוה):** Legally permissible but exposes the tenant to severe financial/legal danger.


* 🟡 **Low Risk (לא נוח / סביר):** Minor inconveniences not worth jeopardizing a good landlord relationship over.




* **Interactive Menu System:** Allows users to dynamically select which risk tiers to include in their response to the landlord.


* **Smart Drafting Engine:** Generates a polite, non-confrontational, and legally sound Hebrew draft for the landlord, complete with alternative clause wording.



---

## 📚 Built-in Knowledge Base

The agent cross-references contract clauses with official Israeli laws and guidelines, using direct triggers for israeli law.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works (Workflow)

```
[Phase 1: Intake] ➔ User uploads lease contract & notes on the contract
       ▼
[Phase 2: Review] ➔ Clause-by-clause evaluation & risk categorization (🔴, 🟠, 🟡)
       ▼
[Phase 3: Menu]   ➔ User selects which risk levels to address
       ▼
[Phase 4: Draft]  ➔ Agent requests missing info & generates a professional Hebrew message to the landlord.

```

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This agent functions as an advisory AI assistant and does not provide binding legal representation or replace a licensed attorney.
